Who We Are
About A.C.T.

Allied Construction Team — A.C.T. — is a student-run construction and home services company based in Dayton, Ohio. We started with a simple idea: learn real skills, do real work, and build something that matters in our community.

Right now we offer home services, maintenance, and lawn care. But the goal is bigger — roofing, landscaping, electrical, and more — each team carrying the A.C.T. name and the same standard of honest work we're building today.

We're early. We're growing. And we're not stopping.
